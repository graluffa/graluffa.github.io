A Little Bit About Me

I'm Grace, a curious UX designer in Chicago.

In my past life as a dietitian, I spent my days listening to people's stories, empathizing with them, and collaborating with them to find suitable solutions. It was during my time with the Women, Infant, and Children (WIC) program that I saw the barriers poor user design and technology created between my clients and their goals. Through User Centered Design, I am able to use my listening skills and empathy to help break that barrier down.

In my off time, you can catch me...

  • Playing Dungeons and Dragons sessions with friends
  • Weightlifting and Yoga stretching
  • Reading Brandon Sanderson Novels
  • Cuddling with my husband and dog
